Calangute Beach




What is special about Calangute Beach? Image result for about calangute beach The Calangute Beach is one of the flagship beaches of Goa and is also famous for the various water sports and activities it hosts. Attracting tourists from all parts, the water sports and activities offered at the Calangute Beach include parasailing, water surfing, banana ride and jet-skiing.

Manali




What makes Manali beautiful?
Try skiing, trekking, rock climbing, rappelling, river crossing, paragliding, ATV Ride, Zorbing, Bonfire, etc. Also, camping in Solang Valley is one of the top things to do in Manali. Best time for camping in Solang Valley: Throughout the year with exception of monsoon.

Kedarnath




The temple, which is dedicated to Lord Shiva, is said to be more than 1,200 years old. It was built by Adi Shankaracharya and is among one of the 12 jyotirlingas in India. A visit to the Kedarnath temple is an integral part of the famous Char Dham Yatra in Uttarakhand. Baba Bhairon Nath protects the Kedarnath templeEvery year the Chota Char Dham Yatra opens for travellers from April to May and the temples are shut for 6 months due to the heavy snowfall.

Hawa Mahal




Hawa Mahal meaning 'palace of winds' is a summer residential palace of the royal family of Jeypore. It was built in 1917-1923 by Maharaja Ram Chandra Dev IV at Beach Road, Visakhapatnam. It is considered one of the most iconic historical buildings of the city. It is owned by the Maharaja of Jeypore.
